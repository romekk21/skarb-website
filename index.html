Secured With Blockchain

Every collectible you discover is more than just a digital item—it's an NFT that's verifiably yours. Skarb makes blockchain simple, secure, and accessible for everyone.

True Ownership

Every collectible you claim is minted on the Polygon blockchain. This means content on Skarb is permanently authentic and decentralized.

Verifiably Yours

Skarb mints collectibles with your wallet signature. You can always verify ownership, even outside of the Skarb app.

Easy Wallet Management

Skarb handles all wallet management and gas fees for you. For full control, you can reveal your private key anytime and view your NFTs elsewhere.
